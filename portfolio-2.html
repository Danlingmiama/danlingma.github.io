Description

The client is a grocery chain. They would like to use their sales data to better understand the business. Therefore, the dashboard break up the sales data into several parts:

1. gives multiple metrics of the overall sales volume and plot the volume trend line

2. shows the store performance variance geographically

3. compares the store performance per product category

4. finds out the relationships between the sales volume and holidays
